# GNSS4Free Projects

## 🚗 CarCompanion

**Android application that transforms your smartphone into a car companion**

An intelligent system that enables your car's infotainment system to leverage your phone's GPS and internet capabilities.

### Current Features
- Automatic HotSpot activation upon Bluetooth connection
- GNSS data broadcasting over local network
- Minimal battery drain through intelligent power management
- Reliable FSM-based architecture

### Status
Active development — Phase 1 complete
