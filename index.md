# 🚗 GNSS4Free Projects

Welcome to GNSS4Free — a collection of open-source projects dedicated to bringing advanced infotainment capabilities to vehicles worldwide.

## 🎯 Our Mission

We create innovative solutions that enhance vehicle functionality through software, making modern infotainment features accessible to all drivers.

## 📱 Featured Project: CarCompanion

**CarCompanion** transforms your smartphone into a digital companion for your car's infotainment system.

### Key Features
- 🌐 Automatic internet sharing via Bluetooth
- 📍 GNSS coordinates broadcasting over local network
- ⚡ High performance with binary protocol
- 🎨 Simple, intuitive interface
- 🆓 Free for personal use
