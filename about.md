## About GNSS4Free

GNSS4Free is an open-source initiative focused on developing software solutions for vehicle infotainment systems.

### Why We Started

Many vehicles, particularly from Chinese manufacturers sold in emerging markets, lack essential infotainment features such as:
- Built-in GPS/navigation
- Telematics modules
- Internet connectivity

Our mission is to provide affordable, open-source solutions that bridge this gap.

### Our Values

- **Open Source** — All our projects are freely available under GPL-3.0
- **User-Centric** — Simple interfaces, no complicated setup
- **Performance** — Efficient code using modern programming techniques
- **Accessibility** — Free for personal use, no hardware modifications needed
